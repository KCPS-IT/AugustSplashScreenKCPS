  • Change Your Wallpaper:
    Go to Settings → Wallpaper → Choose a New Wallpaper. Pick a fun photo or one of Apple's dynamic backgrounds!
  • Set Icon Size:
    In Settings → Display & Brightness → View, choose between Standard and Zoomed to adjust icon size.
  • Change Icon Colors:
    Long press anywhere on the Home Screen then click the Edit button in the top left then hit Customize. Here you can change your icon color and size!

Student Acceptable Use Policy
(Chromebooks & iPads)

  1. Purpose: School devices are provided for educational use only. Students must use Chromebooks and iPads to support learning and follow teacher instructions.
  2. Respectful Use: Treat devices with care. Do not damage, deface, or remove school labels. Keep food and drinks away from devices.
  3. Accountability: Students are responsible for all activity on their assigned device. Do not share your device or account credentials with others.
  4. Appropriate Content: Access only school-appropriate websites, apps, and content. Do not attempt to access, download, or share inappropriate, illegal, or offensive material.
  5. Privacy & Security: Do not attempt to bypass security settings, filters, or install unauthorized software. Do not attempt to access other users' accounts or files.
  6. Digital Citizenship: Communicate respectfully online. Cyberbullying, harassment, or inappropriate language is strictly prohibited.
  7. Media & Photos: Only use the camera and microphone for school assignments with teacher permission. Do not record or photograph others without consent.
  8. Device Care: Bring your device to school fully charged each day. Report any damage, loss, or malfunction to a teacher or IT staff immediately.
  9. Consequences: Violations of this policy may result in loss of device privileges, disciplinary action, and/or financial responsibility for damages.
  10. Agreement: Use of school devices indicates acceptance of this policy and all related district technology guidelines.

For questions or more information, contact your school's technology office or visit the district website.
